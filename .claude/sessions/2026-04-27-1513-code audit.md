### Update — 2026-04-27 ~15:50 — cover-letter design tweaks

**Summary**: Three minor design issues addressed via maintainability-first refactor (per-section style overrides) so future "this section is different" tweaks become a one-line edit at the section module.

**Design issues addressed**:
1. ✅ Vlabel rotation flipped — `BOTTOM_TO_TOP_LEFT_TO_RIGHT` → `TOP_TO_BOTTOM_RIGHT_TO_LEFT` (reads top-to-bottom now).
2. ✅ Joint-obligor actor and guarantor-property vlabels render at 8pt (`SZ_VLABEL_SMALL = 16` half-points) — both contain "Obligado Solidario y Fiador" and were the longest rotated labels.
3. ✅ Empty paragraph inserted after `CONTRATO DE ARRENDAMIENTO.` and after `CARÁTULA.` — `PARTES.` left as-is (not in the user's request).

**Decisions taken** (via /grill-me):

| # | Topic | Resolution |
|---|---|---|
| Q-rotation | Direction | `TOP_TO_BOTTOM_RIGHT_TO_LEFT` (top-to-bottom reading) |
| Q1 | Override mechanism | Option B — per-section explicit overrides via `SectionTableOpts` opts bag, plumbed through `vlabelCell → sectionTable → companySectionTable`. Future "this section is different" knobs land in `SectionTableOpts` (not buried in the cell factory). User confirmed: "very custom styles per section" is fine, sections can have nothing in common. |
| Q2 | Scope | Option B — both joint-obligor actor and guarantor-property sections shrink to 8pt; other sections stay at 9pt. |
| Q3 | New-line interpretation | Option A — literal blank-line empty paragraph (new `blankLine()` helper in `helpers.ts`) between titles. PARTES. unchanged. |
