# **App Name**: HestiaGuard

## Core Features:

- Role-Based Access: User registration and authentication for Owners, Renters, and Staff, with role-based access control.
- Personalized Dashboards: Personalized dashboards for Owners and Renters to view policy status, payment information, and relevant documents.  Staff interface focused on process status and user management.
- Service and Policy Management: Owners can request information and track the status of contracted services. Renters can fill out personal information for policy creation, pay for policies, and download them.
- Backoffice Management: Backoffice interface for Staff to manage users, policies, and system-wide settings.
- Package Presentation: Display package details, including pricing and features, on the public website.  Include CTAs for login/registration or information requests.
- Targeted Landing Pages: Dedicated landing pages on the public site for real estate advisors, property owners, and general company information, with links to Terms & Conditions, Privacy Policy, and Contact Form in the footer.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Warm, inviting orange (#FF7F50) to evoke trust and security, while being approachable.
- Background color: Light, desaturated beige (#F5F5DC) for a clean, neutral backdrop.
- Accent color: Muted coral (#F08080) to draw attention to calls to action and important information.
- Body font: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for a modern and readable experience.
- Headline font: 'Playfair' (serif) for elegant headlines.
- Simple, professional icons to represent policy types, user roles, and key actions.
- Clean, card-based layout to present information clearly and intuitively.
